Account Management

How To Delete Your BloodFit Account

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your BloodFit account and all associated data.

Important Warning

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including your profile, meal plans, and activity history, will be permanently removed.

Step-By-Step Guide

01

Open the BloodFit App

Launch the BloodFit application on your mobile device.

02

Tap On Your Profile Icon

Located at the top corner of the home screen.

03

Go To Settings

Select Settings from your profile menu.

04

Tap On "Delete Account"

Scroll down in Settings and tap the Delete Account option shown in red.

05

Confirm Deletion

A confirmation screen will appear. Tap Delete Account to confirm, or Cancel to go back safely.

What Happens To Your Data

Profile Info — Your name, email, and personal details are permanently deleted

Meal Plans & Activity Logs — All history and personalized plans are removed

Preferences & Settings — All saved preferences are deleted or anonymized

Subscription — You will be logged out immediately and redirected to the welcome screen
